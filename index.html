Eight Witches Hanged


Could your neighbor be one too?
Today, eight people found guilty of witchcraft were hanged. Among those hanged were Martha Corey, Goody Osborne and Tituba. These witches would never have been caught if it weren't for the victims who were brave enough to testify in court. We managed to speak with one of the victims today. "It's really hard work, they keep you in the courtroom all day with these absolutely horrid people. They[the witches] try to come at you with their spirits, to shut you up before you can say anything about them. Especially a few days before the trials, some of them see the future. They try to come at you through the window at night, it's been nothing but nightmares these last few nights." "We're doing out best," said Judge Hathorne in an interview, "we give the witches an option to name other witches to redeem themselves. It really speeds up the process and saves us a lot of effort" The court urges everyone to report any signs and suspicious of witchcraft as quickly as possible.
